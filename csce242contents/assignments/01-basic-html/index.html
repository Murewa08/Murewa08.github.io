How To Plan A Vacation

By Murewa Adebajo CSCE 242 Web Applications

Travelling can be an exciting and overwhelming process. It is important to take your time with the preparation to ensure a smooth and stress-free vacation. Another tip is to understand why you want to go on vacation. Is it for relaxation, adventure, or exploring culture? It will make your preparation easier.

Know the weather and climate of your destination before packing. This will help you pack appropriate clothing and gear for your trip. Do you want a solo or family/friends trip? How long do you have to prepare for this trip? It okay if you need a more time than others to fully prep for it, there is no perfect timeline.

Be sure you have the following:

  1. Travel documents (passport, ID, etc.)
  2. Money (cash, credit cards)
  3. Travel itinerary and maps
